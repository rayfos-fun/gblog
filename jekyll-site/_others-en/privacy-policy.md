# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Rayfos.fun** ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our services (specifically via Google Login).

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide (Google Login)

When you choose to log in using your Google account, we receive the following information via Google, but please note that **we do not publicly display your real identity**:

* **Email Address:** Used as your unique account identifier (Unique ID). It links your custom nickname, comment history, and future game progress saves.
* **Name and Profile Picture:** The Google login process provides this information by default, but we **do not** publicly display your real name or photo in the comments section. The system assigns you a default nickname (or one you set yourself), and only that nickname is displayed publicly.

We **do not** collect your password. The login verification process is handled entirely by Google.

### B. Automatically Collected Information (Google Analytics)

We use **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics may collect:

* Your IP address (anonymized where possible).
* Browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1.  **Account Identification and Nickname Management:** Matching your Google account with your website nickname to ensure you can comment or change your nickname using the same identity.
2.  **Game Progress Saving (Future Feature):** To store game scores or progress when we launch web games in the future.
3.  **Service Improvement:** Analyzing website traffic to improve user experience.
4.  **Communication:** We will **never** use your email to send marketing messages.

## 4. Use of Cookies

We use Cookies to maintain your login status and support Google Analytics functions.

* **Essential Cookies:** Necessary for the login system to function.
* **Analytics Cookies:** Used by Google Analytics to track website usage.

You can disable Cookies through your browser settings, but this may prevent you from logging in and posting comments.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

* **Google Identity Services (OAuth):** For user authentication.
* **Google Analytics:** For traffic analysis.

For more information on how these services handle your data, please refer to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

* **Retention:** We retain your comment content and related profile information as long as your comments remain on the website.
* **Deletion:** You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to remove your comments and related data, please contact us at **rayfos.fun@gmail.com**. We will process your request within 30 days.
